Problem: A campaign operating system that keeps the original idea strong enough to earn results at every stage. Every handover — from strategy to creative, creative to channel, channel to gate — is checked against the idea's integrity anchor. Nothing advances until a specific consumer behaviour signal fires. Each stage compounds the idea's strength toward a business outcome. Not a tracker. Not a brief tool. The kitchen nobody built.
For: CMOs and Brand Directors managing 5–10 agencies across multi-channel campaigns — any industry — under CFO pressure to prove ROI but with no system connecting their campaign idea to a measurable business outcome.
Core things to track: Clarity Statement (integrity anchor), ICS score per dimension, per-stage brief drift vs. original anchor, consumer behaviour gate signals (saves rate, search intent lift, retail velocity, repeat purchase rate), gate status per stage, Kill Switch triggers, weekly business outcome vs. baseline.
The one workflow (must work v1): An idea enters with a locked Clarity Statement → a Stage 1 brief is issued → consumer behaviour signals are tracked against pre-defined thresholds → when the threshold fires, the gate opens → Stage 2 brief is issued from the same anchor. The idea compounded. It didn't drift.
Success in a week: One live campaign has a locked Clarity Statement, pre-defined gate signals with real measurement thresholds, and three execution partners briefed from the same anchor — with drift visible before it becomes a problem.
Not v1: Automated analytics pull, AI brief generation, agency management portal, revenue attribution modelling, campaign duplication, failure mode library, enterprise team permissions, intake forms.

Build focus: Keep it a lean MVP: signups plus the single core action, nothing extra — built to test demand fast.
